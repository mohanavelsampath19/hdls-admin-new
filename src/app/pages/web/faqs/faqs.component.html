Frequently Asked Questions

General Booking Questions

Q: How do i make a reservation?
A: You can make a reservation by selecting your preferred dates, choosing a room, and following the on-screen instructions to complete the booking process. A confirmation email will be sent to you once the booking is successful.
Q: Can I make a reservation without a credit card?
A: Most hotels require a credit card to guarantee your booking. However, some hotels may accept bookings with alternative payment methods. Please check the payment options during the booking process.
Q: How do I know if my reservation is confirmed?
A: After completing your booking, you will receive a confirmation email with your reservation details. If you do not receive this email, please check your spam folder or contact our customer support.

Room and Amenities

Q: What amenities are included with my room?
A: Room amenities vary depending on the hotel and room type. You can find a list of amenities on the room details page during the booking process.
Q: Can I request a specific room or floor?
A: You can make special requests, such as a specific room or floor, during the booking process. While the hotel will do its best to accommodate your request, it cannot be guaranteed.

Check-In and Check-Out

Q: What time is check-in and check-out?
A: Check-in and check-out times vary by hotel. Generally, check-in is 3.00 pm onwards and check-out is by 12:00 pm. Please refer to your booking confirmation for the specific times.
Q: Can I request early check-in or late check-out?
A: Early check-in and late check-out requests are subject to availability and may incur additional charges. Please contact the hotel directly to make these arrangements.

Loyalty Program and Discount

Q: Do you offer a loyalty program?
A: Yes, we offer a loyalty program where you can earn points for every booking. Points can be redeemed for discounts on future stays. Sign up during the booking process or log in to your account to start earning points.
Q: Are there any discounts available?
A: We offer various discounts throughout the year, including special rates for members, early bookings, and last-minute deals. Please check our app for current promotions.
